About Cash and Care.

Our mission and vision:

The sole purpose of making this is to share my journey of learning how to invest money into stocks and making this a profitable side-hustle that I can do. But this isn’t just about me though, it’s about making this information accessible for all of us. Now, think about this: what if we actually understood how to make our money grow instead of just spending it? The goal here isn’t to make a million dollars overnight but to take something that seems complicated and show that it is completely possible to do. Schools teach us a lot of stuff, but they don't tell us how to make smart financial decisions in the real world and because of this, we miss out on the opportunities to start making money at a young age.
